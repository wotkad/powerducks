PowerDucks secured a partnership with Gamer Network and a global video games publisher, Square Enix, sponsoring an AAA video game launch, Just Cause 3, in 2015:
TORNADO ENERGY and Just Cause 3 dual branded microsite launched three months ahead of EGX live event
Game Network and partner platforms branded takeovers run during the three months before the live event
Online competition launched on the branded microsite and led to from media platforms such as the Gamer Network, Square Enix, Just Cause 3 website and social media channels
Results

We created an experience that immersed every visitor of the event as well as online audience through 75SQM of 3 TORNADO ENERGY single branded zones and 480 SQM of TORNADO ENERGY and Just Cause 3 dual branded tournament zones with 64 tournament seats in total, as well as digital campaign held in partnership with the Gamer Network and Just Cause 3 with total media reach >9M

We provided every gamer at the event with a free can of TORNADO ENERGY.
